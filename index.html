About

I'm a UX Designer and Brand Strategist with 10 years of design experience, mostly in international nonprofits and cross-cultural contexts. My background is visual design, but the problems I've worked on have usually been less about how things look and more about whether people can actually understand and trust what they're looking at. The work I'm most proud of sits in two categories. Client projects: a rebrand that unified a fragmented nonprofit across 17 countries, and the first self-service laundromat launch in Northeast India, designed for a market where the concept was completely unfamiliar. Independent builds: MyRateKit (myratekit.com), a free financial toolkit for freelancers across the US, UK, Australia, and Canada, and EntryRight (entryright.lovable.app), a visa stay planner for digital nomads. Both taken from research to live product as solo builds. Portfolio: alphonseriang.com Open to UX Designer and Product Designer roles, hybrid or remote.
